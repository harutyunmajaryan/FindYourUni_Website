About FindYourUni

FindYourUni is a university matchmaking website designed to help students find universities that best suit their needs based on their personal preferences.

Choosing a university can be overwhelming. There are a ton of rankings based on countless metrics that can lead to a seemingly never-ending search. We aim to simplify the process and save you time by narrowing your list of universities down using what matters most to you!

How our algorithm works

We use a K-Nearest Neighbour algorithm to compare your grades and preferences against a dataset of university information to give you personalized results.

How To Get Started:

  • Enter Your Grades

    • Enter your grades (A levels or IB)

  • Choose Your Course

    • Enter the course you are interested in applying for.

  • Set Your Preferences

    • Choose factors such as student satisfaction, teaching quality, and employability, then use the sliders to indicate how important each factor is to you.

  • Get Your Results!

    • Receive a ranked list of personalized university recommendations tailored to your needs.

Frequently Asked Questions

How is my data used?

We do not share your personal information. Grades and preferences are used only to generate university recommendations.

What grades can I enter?

Our website supports A-Levels and IB to help us find universities with similar grade requirements by converting to UCAS tariff points. If you like a particular course, make sure to check the university website to confirm the grade requirements are suitable.

Do I need to make an account?

Our tool can still be used without logging in, but you will not be able to save courses to your shortlist without an account.

Are my results guaranteed?

No. FindYourUni provides recommendations based on the information you provide. The results are only a guide to help narrow down your search and is not a definitive ranking. We recommend conducting further research on each university. If you want to access the websites to the courses, they can be found when you expand on a university in the table.

How do we gather our Data?

The following metrics are gathered from HESA's Discover Uni Dataset :

  • Student Satisfaction
  • Graduate Outcomes
  • Average Entry Tariff Points
  • Cost of Living was obtained by mapping postcodes to cities and using the Natwest Student Living Index 2025. From this we gave each city an affordability score
  • Research Quality from Research Excellence Framework.
  • OFS was also used to differentiate between universities and other higher education providers.
