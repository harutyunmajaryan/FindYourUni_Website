How It Works

01 Select your preferences

Rate each of the factors in deciding your course from 1-10. We consider:

  • Student Satisfaction
  • Earning Prospects
  • Cost of Living
  • Graduate Employment Rate
  • Student-to-Staff Ratio
  • Research Quality
  • Campus Facilities
  • Course Flexibility

02 Filter your options

Filter the courses displayed on the results page by university location, course category, course name, and the grade of the average accepted student. Note: The average accepted grade is calculated from students' UCAS tariff points, so may not be directly analogous to your A-Level grades.

03 Get instant results

Get your top course options ranked in order of closest mathematical fit to your preferences. Use the link provided to start your extra research and see if a course is really for you! Save promising courses onto your shortlist. Enable cookies to have your shortlist saved between visits.

Disclaimer:

Our website is designed to get you started on your research, to identify what courses you might want to start researching on your quest to find the best course for you.
We don't recommend that you simply apply to the top pick the algorithm gives you without prior research into how much you like the course, and your likelihood of being accepted for it.
